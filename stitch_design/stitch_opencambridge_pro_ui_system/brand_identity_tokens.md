# OpenCamBridge Brand Asset Pack & Design Tokens

## 1. Brand Identity
OpenCamBridge is a premium, open-source technical utility. The brand identity is built on transparency, privacy, and technical precision.

### Logo Concept: "The Lens Bridge"
The icon combines a camera aperture/lens with bridge-like network waves. It symbolizes the bridge between your mobile hardware and your desktop workspace.

## 2. Design Tokens

- **OLED Surface:** `#121317` (Primary Background)
